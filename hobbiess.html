About Hobbies & Interests

My Hobbies & Interests

Food

I really enjoy cooking different Filipino dishes like Adobo, Sinigang, Pinakbet, Nilaga, and Sisig. These dishes are always a hit at home, and I love how they bring my family together. Every time I cook them, it feels like I’m sharing a piece of our culture.

Favorite Anime

Anime has always been one of my favorite hobbies. Shows like One Piece, Demon Slayer, Attack on Titan, Slam Dunk, and Dragon Ball have made such an impact on me. I love how they mix action, friendship, and adventure in ways that make you feel connected to the characters.

Music

Music has always been a big part of my life. Bands like Parokya ni Edgar, Eraserheads, Silent Sanctuary, Orange and Lemons, and Apo Hiking Society have shaped the way I see life. Their songs speak to me in a way that no other music does, and I always find myself going back to them.

Games

I’ve been passionate about playing games like Call of Duty, Mobile Legends, Terraria, and Minecraft for years. They’re not just fun, but they’re also a great way to connect with friends and unwind after a busy day. Each game brings a different experience, and I enjoy them all in their own unique way.
